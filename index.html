Jian Liu (刘健)

PhD, Division of Computer Science and Engineering, Louisiana State University. [jliu83@lsu.edu] [BITS Lab]
Jian Liu is currently a Ph.D. candidate student majored in Computer Science at Louisiana State University. His research interests focus on flash-based storage/caching systems, data deduplication, object-based storage, etc. His research works have been published in major conferences and journals, such as VLDB, ICDCS, MSST, TPDS.
